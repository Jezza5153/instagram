# OffertesVoorJou — Brand Guide & Philosophy

## Core Identity
"Een gratis plek waar je snel meerdere vakmensen kunt vinden en offertes kunt vergelijken, zonder dat bedrijven moeten betalen voor elke lead."

## Philosophy
- If vakmensen succeed, the platform succeeds
- Anti-lead-platform mentality (no pay-per-lead, ever)
- Built WITH vakmensen, not against them
- Current system is broken → we're the fix

## Values
1. **Transparency** — no hidden costs, tricks, or lead auctions
2. **Fairness** — vakmensen should not pay for bad leads
3. **Simplicity** — consumers find a professional within minutes
4. **Local trust** — real businesses, real reviews, real work

## Tone of Voice
- Honest, direct, human, helpful
- Use **je/jij** (informal)
- NOT corporate, NOT marketing fluff
- **Love**: gratis, vergelijken, betrouwbaar, lokaal, snel
- **Avoid**: goedkoop, de beste, nummer 1, exclusief, premium

## Priority Professions
1. Aannemers (high demand)
2. Schilders (converts well)
3. Cateraars (event-based)
4. Makelaars (high value, lower volume)

## Target Cities (Phase 1)
Amersfoort, Utrecht, Amsterdam, Randstad

## Founder Story
Jeremy — chef background → entrepreneur → frustrated with lead platforms → building something better. This authenticity is the brand's superpower.
